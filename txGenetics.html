Tx Genetics

A self-help website

Situation

    The client contacted Recursive Thinking requesting a website to help share her story. The client wanted to share her story and how she was able to self-cure herselfr from a painful genetic disorder.

Task

    My task was to develop the front-end for the client centered around connecting the client with other people who are experiencing the same symptoms as her. The product needed user/admin privledges, blog submission, social features, a community news feed, and data/analytics.

Action

    I personally owned the user dashboard implementation with successful blog submission posts to the back-end. Also, was a key driver in implementing new features to increase user experience.

Results

    Product still in production. Will update upon completion.

Technologies used

    React.JS, Redux, AWS-Serverless, JavaScript, HTML5/CSS3
